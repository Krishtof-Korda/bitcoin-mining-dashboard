Wallet tracking (optional)

Paste the payout address(es) your pool sends rewards to. The dashboard then shows the actual BTC received on-chain alongside the run-rate estimate — in the KPI row, the recoupment legend, and the figures above. (Pool earnings sit off-chain until the pool sends a payout, so this can lag the estimate.) Addresses are read-only — they can never spend funds — and are saved in this browser only (localStorage). They are never uploaded or committed anywhere. Note: the total counts everything received by these addresses, not just mining payouts.

Methodology

Live data: CoinGecko (BTC/USD) and Blockchain.info (network difficulty), refreshed every 60 seconds. Daily yield = fleet hashrate × 86,400 s × block reward ÷ (difficulty × 2³²), net of the pool fee, using the contract parameters set above. On-chain wallet totals (when tracking is enabled) come from Blockchain.info with mempool.space as fallback — read-only lookups of public addresses. Estimates only — actual pool payouts vary with luck, fees, and difficulty adjustments. Not financial or tax advice.
